# Employee Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition data to identify the key factors influencing employee turnover. The analysis combines data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), business insights, and an interactive Power BI dashboard to help HR teams make data-driven decisions for improving employee retention.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition trends.
* Identify factors contributing to employee turnover.
* Visualize HR metrics through an interactive dashboard.
* Generate business insights and recommendations.

---

## Dataset

* **Dataset:** IBM HR Employee Attrition Dataset
* **Total Employees:** 1,470
* **Features:** 31 employee-related attributes
* **Target Variable:** Attrition (Yes/No)

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Cleaning

* Checked for missing values
* Removed duplicate records
* Verified data types
* Prepared data for analysis

### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Employee distribution analysis
* Attrition analysis by department
* Attrition by gender
* Attrition by business travel
* Attrition by education field
* Job satisfaction analysis
* Work-life balance analysis
* Monthly income analysis
* Correlation analysis

### 3. Power BI Dashboard

The dashboard includes:

* Total Employees
* Employees Left
* Attrition Rate
* Average Monthly Income
* Average Years at Company

Visualizations include:

* Attrition by Department
* Attrition by Job Role
* Attrition by OverTime
* Attrition by Business Travel
* Attrition by Education Field
* Job Satisfaction vs Attrition
* Work Life Balance vs Attrition

Interactive filters:

* Department
* Gender
* Job Role
* Business Travel
* Marital Status
---
## Key Insights

* Employees working overtime showed higher attrition.
* Certain departments experienced higher employee turnover.
* Business travel frequency influenced attrition rates.
* Employees with lower job satisfaction were more likely to leave.
* Monthly income and years at the company showed relationships with employee retention.
